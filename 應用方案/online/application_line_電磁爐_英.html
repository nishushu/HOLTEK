Holtek has released induction cooker solutions with the advantages of reduced external components, superior performance and high reliability. These solutions using Holtek’s touch ICs show excellent performance in induction cooker environment where there is often excessive electrical noise and interference.

To help users develop related products rapidly, Holtek provides a block diagram, application circuits, PCB, component list and other technical information including the complete MCU code. To learn more about the competitive advantages of our induction cooker solution, click “Learn more about our solutions”.
