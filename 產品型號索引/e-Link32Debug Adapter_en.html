e-Link32 Debug Adapter

The e-Link32 is a debug adapter for Holtek's 32-bit microcontrollers allowing users to program and debug their programs on their target boards. By using the e-Link32 together with the Keil µVision4 IDE or IAR EWARM IDE, users are provided with a suite of development tools for rapid MCU product development. Refer to the "e-Link32 Quick Start" to familiarize users with Holtek e-Link32 Debug Adapter.
