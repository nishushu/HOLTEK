HOLTEK新推出BS87B12A-3/BS87D20A-3觸控OPA Flash MCU

09/19/2016

關鍵字：BS87B12A-3 BS87D20A-3

Holtek在需要觸控加OPA功能的小家電產品上，繼BS87C16A-3之後，再度推出BS87B12A-3與BS87D20A-3。本產品適合需求20個觸控按鍵以下的小家電產品，例如電飯煲、電飯鍋、電茶壺、電陶爐、微波爐、電磁爐、鬆餅機、抽油煙機，觸控台燈，觸控門鎖，體脂計等應用領域產品。


BS87xxxA-3與BS86xxxA-3最主要的功能差別，在於BS87系列多了一組OPA與Comparator。BS87xxxA-3所內建OPA功能用來放大環境中微小的類比信號，不但可以應用於過電流/過電壓偵測，也可外接Shock Sensor以提供系統保護機制。全系列IC所內建的LED/LCD Driver均俱備4段電流輸出控制，可直推LED/LCD不需要外掛限流電阻或三極管。BS87系列還整合SPI/I2C/UART三種標準介面傳輸功能。12-bit ADC可用於擷取外界環境中的類比訊號，例如溫度、濕度或其他電壓訊號量測等。使用BS87xxxA-3可以大幅簡化系統零組件數目，降低生產成本及增加產品可靠度。


BS87B12A-3提供20-Pin NSOP及24-Pin SOP兩種封裝型式，BS87D20A-3提供28-Pin SOP及44-Pin LQFP兩種封裝型式，其中24/28-pin SOP封裝腳位完全相容於BS86xxxA-3。

Holtek BS87B12A-3 / BS87D20A-3 New Touch + OPA Flash MCUs

09/19/2016

Keyword： BS87B12A-3 / BS87D20A-3

Following the successful previous release of its BC87C16A-3 device, Holtek is now delighted to announce the release of its new Touch Key Flash MCUs, the BS87B12A-3 and BS87D20A-3, which include an internal operational amplifier. These devices are suitable for use in small appliances which require 20 or less touch keys, such as rice cookers, electric kettles, electric ceramic stoves, microwaves, induction cookers, waffle makers, kitchen ventilators, touch lamps, touch door locks, body fat meters and other related products.


Compared with the BS86xxxA-3 series, the new BS87xxxA-3 series of devices include an internal OPA and comparator functions. The integrated OPA function can be used to amplify small environmental analog signals, such as those from external sensors, to provide system protection mechanisms. An example of such a measurement could be from an external shock sensor. Another feature of this series is the internal LED/LCD driver which supports 4 segments with current output control. This is able to drive LED/LCD displays directly without requiring additional external current-limiting resistors or transistors. The BS87 series of devices also include SPI, I2C and UART interfaces as well as an internal 12-bit A/D converter which can be used to capture analog signals from the external environment, such as temperature, humidity or other voltage signals. With their high level of functional integration the new devices can greatly reduce the need for external components thus reducing costs as well as improving product reliability.


The BS87B12A-3 is available in 20-pin NSOP and 24-SOP package types while the BS87D20A-3 is available in 28-pin SOP and 44-pin LQFP package types. The 24-pin SOP and 28-pin SOP package types are fully pin-compatible with the BS86xxxA-3 devices.

200mA PFM Synchronous Step-up DC/DC Converter

The HT77xxSA devices are a high efficiency PFM synchronous step-up DC-DC converter series which are designed to operate with both wire wound chip power inductors and also with multi-layered chip power inductors. The device series have the advantages of extremely low start-up voltage as well as high output voltage accuracy. Being manufactured using CMOS technology ensures ultra low supply current. Because of their higher operating frequency, up to 500 kHz, the devices have the benefits of requiring smaller outline type lower value external inductors and capacitors. The higher operating frequency also offers the advantages of much reduced audio frequency noise. The devices require only three external components to provide a fixed output voltage of 2.7V, 3.0V, 3.3V, 3.7V or 5.0V.

The HT77xxSA devices include an internal oscillator, PFM control circuit, driver transistor, reference voltage unit and a high speed comparator. They employ pulse frequency modulation techniques, to obtain minimum supply current and ripple at light output loading. These devices are available in space saving TO92, SOT89, SOT23 and SOT23-5 packages. For SOT23-5 package types, they also include an internal chip enable function to reduce power consumption when in the shutdown mode.
